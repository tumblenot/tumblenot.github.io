Quick Geography Breakdown

  • The Tourist Epicenter: Intercity Hotel Shanghai sits dead-center in the absolute heart of downtown Huangpu. It places you within a 5 to 15-minute walk of both the East Nanjing Road pedestrian street and the iconic Bund waterfront.
  • The Northern Outlier: SAVHE Hotel sits just across the Suzhou Creek from the center, up in the North Bund area. It's excellent for historic, local neighborhood vibes and skyline views, but sits slightly apart from the western shopping districts.
  • The Central Core: Donghu Collection and Atour Jing'an are positioned further west in the prime historic and commercial heart of the city—nestled into walkable, tree-lined streets filled with cafes, boutiques, and nightlife.
  • The Southwest Corridor: A.T.House (Xujiahui) and UrCove (West Bund) stretch toward the southwest. Xujiahui is a massive retail and transit hub, while the West Bund is a newer, spacious arts district positioned right along the modern riverside.
