Local favourites (Oct = peak crab roe season)
Tips: October is peak season for crab roe & hairy crab. Popular crab restaurants may require booking weeks ahead.

French Concession / Jing’an (multiple branches) • Best: 7–10am (before 8am)
¥8–13

Dinner
These are the group dinner options for the 4 nights (Main + Alternatives), from your dinner sheet. Booking windows are important in October.
Sat 11 Oct (Welcome dinner)
Budget: ~S$50–70/pax • Reserve: 1 week ahead (WeChat mini-program)
Peking duck
  • Main: DaDong 大董 — iapm Mall, 999 Huaihai Middle Road, Xuhui
  • Why: Leaner duck; better than Quan Ju De
  • Alternatives: Siji Minfu 四季民福 (Hong Kong Plaza, 283 Middle Huaihai Road, Huangpu) • Quan Ju De 全聚德 (classic, more touristy)
Sun 12 Oct (Free & Easy dinner)
Budget: ~S$40–55/pax • Reserve: 3–4 weeks ahead (request private room)
Hairy crab season
  • Main: Ye Shanghai 夜上海 — Infinitus Plaza, 168 Hubin Rd, Huangpu
  • Why: Michelin Bib; elegant 1930s setting; great for group.
  • Alternatives: Old Jesse 老吉士 (41 Tianping Rd, Xuhui; very local) • Sue Dim Sum 苏小柳 (casual, kid-friendly)
Mon 13 Oct (Post-Hangzhou)
Budget: ~S$60–80/pax • Reserve: ⚠️ 3 weeks ahead (Oct demand extreme)
Peak crab
  • Main: Cheng Long Hang 成隆行 — 616 Fuzhou Rd, Huangpu
  • Why: Top pick; Shanghai’s #1 hairy crab restaurant; October peak.
  • Alternatives: Ren He Guan 人和馆 (407 Zhaojiabang Rd, Xuhui; swap if full) • Xiao Tao Yuan 小桃园 (simple comfort)
Tue 14 Oct (Farewell dinner)
Budget: ~S$47/pax (¥250) • Reserve: ⚠️ 3 weeks ahead (pre-order pork belly; request 2 private rooms)
Michelin
  • Main: Ren He Guan 人和馆 — 407 Zhaojiabang Rd, Xuhui
  • Why: Michelin; pre-order hongshaorou via WeChat; old Shanghai retro interior; 2 private rooms.
  • Alternatives: Ye Shanghai 夜上海 (Infinitus Plaza, 168 Hubin Rd, Huangpu) • House of Rong 荣府宴 (Taizhou cuisine)

Hangzhou Day Trip (Day 3) — what to eat
Ask the guide to choose a non-touristy restaurant near the Tea Village / West Lake area.
Longjing Tea Shrimp (龙井虾仁)
Freshwater shrimp stir-fried with Dragon Well tea leaves
¥88–168
  • Tip: Zhiwei Guan 知味观 chain is reliable, best near Longjing Tea Village.
Dongpo Pork (东坡肉)
Classic Hangzhou braised pork belly
¥68–98
  • Tip: One portion often shares well (2–3 kids or 1–2 adults); try restaurants near XiXi Wetland.
West Lake Vinegar Fish (西湖醋鱼)
Whole fish in sweet-sour vinegar glaze
¥68–128
  • Tip: Some places do boneless version; avoid Hefang Street (tourist trap).
Beggar’s Chicken (叫花鸡)
Lotus-leaf wrapped chicken baked in clay
¥138–198
  • Tip: 1 chicken feeds 4; theatrical reveal; order ahead (30 minutes).
